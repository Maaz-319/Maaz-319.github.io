How to Play

Welcome to Space Shooter! Your mission is to survive and eliminate enemies while dodging obstacles.

Game Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys or W, A, S, D
  • Fire: Spacebar
  • Pause/Resume: P
  • Start Game: Enter
  • Change Attack Type: 1
  • Change Attack Type: 2
  • Change Attack Type: 3

Game Mechanics

  • Avoid incoming meteors and enemy fire.
  • Survive gamma ray hazards that appear randomly.
  • Collect power-ups to upgrade your weapons and gain shields.
  • Fight increasingly challenging enemies as the game progresses.
  • Try to beat your high score!
